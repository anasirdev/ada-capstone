Let's get started!

If you haven't already done so, you will need to get a .ged file to upload. Log in to your favorite genealogy site where you already have a family tree built. For instructions on how to do that, click one of the buttons below.

downloading tree from ancestry
While viewing your tree, click the drop-down and choose tree settings. On the right, near the bottom, click on DOWNLOAD YOUR GEDCOM FILE. Save the file to your computer.
downloading tree from myheritage
While viewing your tree, click the drop-down and choose tree settings. On the right, near the bottom, click on DOWNLOAD YOUR GEDCOM FILE. Save the file to your computer.
downloading tree from familysearch
While viewing your tree, click the drop-down and choose tree settings. On the right, near the bottom, click on DOWNLOAD YOUR GEDCOM FILE. Save the file to your computer.
